About Us

Our Story & Mission

Kithauli Coaching Center was founded over a decade ago with a singular mission: to provide quality education to every student in and around Kithauli village, regardless of their background. What started as a small tuition class has grown into a trusted academic institution serving 1,200+ students.

Our teaching philosophy is rooted in concept clarity, regular practice, and individualized attention. Small batch sizes ensure that no student is left behind, and our experienced faculty brings both expertise and passion to every class.
